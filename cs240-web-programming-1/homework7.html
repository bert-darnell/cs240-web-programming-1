Types of Artificial Inteligence

NOTE: Click on one of the icons in the image to learn more about it.

1. Machine Learning

Machine learning enables computers to learn from data and make predictions or decisions.

2. Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence simulates human intelligence in machines for various tasks.

3. Neural Networks

Neural networks are machine learning models inspired by the human brain.

4. Natural Language Processing

NLP allows computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language.

5. Robotics

Robotics involves designing machines, often with AI, for physical tasks.
